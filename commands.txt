# Available Commands

- `/play_resistance` — Start a new game of *The Resistance*.  
- `/leave_game` — Leave the current game.  
- `/propose_team <@user1> <@user2> ...` — Propose a team for the mission.  
- `/vote_mission success|fail` — Vote on the mission outcome.  
- `/role` — Check your role in the game.  
- `/rules` — Display the rules of *The Resistance*.
- `/patch_note` — Display the latest patch note.
